Objective

  • Establish

    To establish a consistent visual aesthetic and maintain a high quality of deliverables for the DealerOn brand.

  • Provide

    To provide an easily accessible library of assets ready for production to all or any persons creating web or print products for DealerOn.

  • Create

    To create a DealerOn review panel and process which will ensure quality is maintained for DealerOn web or print products.

  • Commit

    If they don't follow the guidelines, they get dead.

Process

  1. Creative Brief

    Receive direction and scope of current "focus item" from panel(TBD).

  2. R & D

    Conduct thorough research of proven effective solutions and potential improvements, provide examples and statistics. Legibility, trends, marketing, etc.

  3. Initial

    Develop sketches, drafts, etc. of visual aesthetic and its application.

  4. Revise

    Per panel review revise drafted material accordingly and proceed to user testing.

  5. Q & A

    Use case will vary per "focus item", require technical and non-technical employees to create mock materials, document ease of use, etc.

  6. Initial Final

    Provide per "focus item" application guidelines and appropriate deliverables: file format, pantone etc.
